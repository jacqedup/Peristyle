  • Child Custody
  • Community Property

Ledbetter Law will protect your rights and fight for what you deserve.

DCFS Cases
  • Department of Children and Family Services(DCFS)
  • Child In Need of Care

Family and Personal Life

Scott Ledbetter was born in Dallas, TX. He moved to Louisiana as a child and has been living in St. Charles Parish. He is married to Leslie Benz Ledbetter and has two children. His hobbies include CrossFit, hunting, fishing and camping. Scott is also an avid LSU football fan; on Saturdays in the fall, he will either be in the woods, on the water or watching an LSU game. Being the first generation college student, Scott Ledbetter knows how to work to achieve his goals. He believes that attorneys should be a lifetime advocate for his clients. Because of this, Scott strives to be there for his clients to help with every one of their legal needs.

Legal Experience

Scott began working in the legal field in 2014 while in Law School. He worked at Collins and Arnove, a bankruptcy firm in Plano, TX.

Following law school Scott worked as a fellow for the Southeast Louisiana Legal Services. His fellowship primarily dealt with representing children in the foster care system and assisting self-represented litigants at the Lafourche Parish Self Help Desk. However, he also assisted in the preparation of wills, Power of Attorney documents, Provisional Custody documents, and various Family Law issues.

In January 2018, he decided to venture out on his own to expand his experience in the practice of law. He now handles cases dealing with Divorce, custody, child support, personal injury, workers compensation, medical malpractice, and Department of Child and Family Services.
